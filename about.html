About

Dr. Ranjit Podder is an Associate Professor at the Department of English Education, Govt. Teachers' Training College, Dhaka. As an accomplished educational researcher, he has published 32 research papers and serves as the editor of the Bangladesh Teacher Education Journal (BTEJ). His research areas include English Language Teaching (ELT), Assessment, Curriculum Development, Learning Theories, and Adult Education. He is also deeply involved in mentoring, feedback, and Assessment for Learning (AfL).

Education

  • Ph.D. in Education, Dhaka University
  • M.Phil from the Institute of Bangladesh Studies, Rajshahi University
  • MA in English and BA Honours from Dhaka University
  • Master of Education from Canterbury University, New Zealand
  • MA in Education from Nottingham University, Malaysia
  • Postgraduate Diploma in Education from UC, New Zealand
